# ILO 1.6 Presentation

The student demonstrates the ability to convert project findings into a coherent and structured presentation. The student is able to communicate the project findings in a professional manner tailored to the audience. 

|pre-requisites|A: 2 points|B: 2 points|C: 2 points|D: 2 points|E: 2 points|
|--|--|--|--|--|--|
|The student submits a complete learning log, work log, and self-assessment rubric. The student exhibits professional behaviour during DataLab sessions.|The student creates well-designed slides where all content is easy to read, and visual elements such as tables, figures, and images are clear and appropriately sized. The student avoids excessive text, ensuring slides are concise and focused. Proper citations are included when needed on individual slides, and each slide has a clear title and is numbered for reference.|The student demonstrates thorough preparation, starts on time, adheres to the time limit, and delivers a well-paced and complete presentation.|The student responds to questions clearly and accurately, demonstrating a solid understanding of the topic and providing thoughtful, well-reasoned answers.|The student delivers the presentation confidently, maintaining eye contact, using clear language, and engaging the audience throughout.|The student organizes the presentation logically, ensuring that the problem, approach, results and interpretation thereof are easy to follow and well-explained.|
